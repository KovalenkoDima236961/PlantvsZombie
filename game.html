About the game

1.Start the game "Plant vs Zombie".
2.Choose one of the proposed game levels in which you will defend yourself against zombies by installing different defenders.
3.The game is controlled with the mouse.
4.The player can place plants on the lawn by hovering over an empty cell with a black square to summon additional defenders, hover over the top bar and select from the options.
5.When zombies appear, your defenders will automatically attack them. Place your defenders strategically to stop the zombies before they reach your home.
6.For every zombie you destroy, you get money that can be used to buy new defenders and improve existing ones.
